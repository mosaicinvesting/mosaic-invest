Closed positions

Exits are lessons, not failures.

We publish every position we exit, including the ones that lost money. A small loss that teaches the whole club something is a win we get to keep forever.

How the money works

Contributions to Mosaic are donations to the club's shared portfolio, not personal investments: members don't own individual shares and contributions aren't returned. Members research, pitch, and vote together to direct the portfolio as a learning exercise, and an adult officer executes every trade. No student ever handles the money, and nobody has to contribute to participate.
