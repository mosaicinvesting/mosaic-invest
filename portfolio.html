Closed positions

Exits are lessons, not failures.

We publish every position we exit, including the ones that lost money. A small loss that teaches the whole club something is a win we get to keep forever.

How the money works

Contributions to Mosaic are donations to the club's shared portfolio, not personal investments: members don't own individual shares and contributions aren't returned. No student handles the money, and nobody has to contribute to participate.

Submit a pitch

A reviewer checks that your pitch has a real thesis, named risks, and some valuation logic before it goes to the club. It never decides whether to buy, it just makes sure the pitch is complete. If anything's thin, you'll get specific notes to revise, not a rejection.

Want a vote on the next pick?

Every member researches, pitches, and votes. Open to high schoolers 18 and under, anywhere in the world.
