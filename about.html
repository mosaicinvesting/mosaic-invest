About Mosaic

Built so no teen has to learn money the hard way.

Mosaic is a student-led nonprofit. We exist because financial education is missing from most classrooms, and we think the best way to fix that is to let teens learn by actually investing, together.

Our mission

Give every teenager the chance to build real financial confidence: through an educational curriculum, a portfolio they help build, and a community that learns together.

Who it's for

High schoolers, 18 and under, anywhere in the world.

You don't need money, experience, or a finance background. You need curiosity and the willingness to show up, learn, and vote.

Mosaic is a fit if you're…

  • A high schooler aged 18 or under
  • Curious about investing and money, at any level
  • Ready to learn alongside other students
