About Mosaic

Built so no teen has to learn money the hard way.

Mosaic is a student-led nonprofit, registered as a nonprofit corporation in Washington State. We exist because financial education is missing from most classrooms, and we think the best way to fix that is to let teens learn by actually investing, together.

Our mission

Give every teenager the chance to build real financial confidence: through an educational curriculum, a portfolio they help build, and a community that learns together.

Our team

The students behind Mosaic.

JT

Founder & CEO

Jayden Tu

Seattle, WA

Jayden started paying attention to the stock market as an elementary schooler. What started as an interest grew into a habit of investing and a passion for how markets work.

The more he learned, he realized how almost none of it was taught in school. He was picking up knowledge on his own time, from the news and his own research. That gap is why he built Mosaic, a place where teens don't just read about investing but actually do it together, with real stakes and a real say.
