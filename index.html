Our Solution

A program where teens learn finance by doing it.

Mosaic pairs a structured curriculum with a real investment portfolio.

Real money, real stakes

Unlike simulators, members direct real dollars. Mosaic provides the capital, so none of it comes out of your pocket. Because every decision has real consequences, the lessons last longer.

Student-driven decisions

Every position starts as a member's pitch and passes a community vote. The portfolio reflects the whole club, not any one person.

Learning meets application

Curriculum and investing live in one program, so every module is put into practice on the club's real portfolio. Pitch it, debate it, vote on it.
